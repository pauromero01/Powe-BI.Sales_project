# Sales report
With an imagined sales dataset, we explore an analytic approach using Power BI.

## Executive summary report
<img width="1250" height="690" alt="Executive summary" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d8e60b98-41ab-44ee-9659-41632087ba2f" />
Sales show a general upward trend with seasonal peaks, especially toward the end of each fiscal year. Bikes dominate revenue (€66.3M), particularly through the "Specialty Bike Shop" channel, followed by Components (€11.8M) and Clothing (€1.8M). Geographically, sales are concentrated in North America and Europe. The "Warehouse" and "Specialty Bike Shop" channels drive the majority of sales across categories. Overall, bikes are the primary revenue driver, with strong performance in specialized retail and key markets.
